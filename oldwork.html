High Fashion Boutique - Space Panning & Furniture Design

Junya Tashiro is a Japanese designer whose clothing adapts to an organic flow and style that matures with age. He has a fresh earthy take, on a common bohemian trend, with mystical feel that will be reflected in to his shop. This boutique is to explore the concept of an organic doll house, where the whimsical mood of the space and clothing transmits on to the client. The space is designed to make clientele feel as if they have entered a secret, private world. Upon entering a person belongs here, it is an inclusive place that one can look forward to returning to each time. A place where the walls are embedded with Victorian details and the overall work brings natures patterns into human design. The colours accent the clothing and the overall feel is tranquil. Tashiro’s clothing is the focal point and the design accents his fashion.
